## 🧠 SEO Reasoning (US & EU Focus)
> These changes improve US/EU rankings by: 
1.  **Increased Keyword Density & Relevance:** Integrating high-volume long-tail keywords directly into crucial on-page elements (title, description, headings, lead, body) signals stronger relevance to search engines for those specific queries, like 'compound interest calculator uk' (trend score 73) and 'percentage calculator increase' (trend score 65).
2.  **Targeted Audience Appeal:** Explicitly mentioning 'US and EU professionals' and specific country contexts like 'UK' helps search engines deliver the content to the correct geographical audience, improving click-through rates from localized searches.
3.  **Improved User Experience (Indirect SEO):** Clearer, keyword-rich headings and descriptive content help users quickly find what they're looking for, potentially reducing bounce rates and increasing time on page, which are positive ranking signals.
4.  **Semantic SEO:** By diversifying keywords around the core topics (e.g., 'conversion tools', 'unit converter app', 'unit converter weight'), the content provides a more comprehensive answer to user queries, helping algorithms understand the breadth of the tool's utility.
5.  **Frontmatter Optimization:** An optimized title and meta description are critical for search engine result page (SERP) visibility and encouraging clicks, directly impacting organic traffic.
